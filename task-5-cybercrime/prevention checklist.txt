Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

1. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
2. Always check the website URL before entering any details.
3. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
4. Never share your OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
5. Be careful while accepting UPI payment requests.
6. Do not download files from untrusted sources.
7. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security.
8. Always log out from public or shared devices.
9. Report any cybercrime immediately on cybercrime.gov.in.
10. Call helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud.
