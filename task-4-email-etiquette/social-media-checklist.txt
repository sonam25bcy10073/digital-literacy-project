Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

 DO’s:
1. Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts.
2. Think before posting anything online.
3. Keep your profiles private whenever possible.
4. Verify information before sharing it.
5. Be respectful and polite in online communication.

 DON’Ts:
1. Do not share your OTP, passwords, or personal details.
2. Do not post private or sensitive information publicly.
3. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages.
4. Do not engage in cyberbullying or harmful comments.
5. Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.
